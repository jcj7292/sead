SEAD Lab was pleased to host a research team from Pratt & Whitney for an on-site visit focused on advancing AI for automation in manufacturing and materials design.

During the visit, SEAD Lab showcased recent progress on:
- Uncertainty-aware deep learning
- AI-accelerated materials design
- Real-time analytics in manufacturing

The Pratt & Whitney team shared perspectives on industrial challenges in quality assurance and digital manufacturing, highlighting strong alignment with SEAD Lab’s ongoing research.

SEAD Lab looks forward to continued discussions and the potential for impactful collaboration with Pratt & Whitney.

Research from the SEAD Lab at Virginia Tech on trustworthy AI for materials characterization has been featured in *Aerospace America*. The work highlights the development of uncertainty-aware machine learning methods that improve the reliability of high-throughput materials analysis.

The research focuses on bridging the gap between simulation and experiment using physics-informed and uncertainty-aware models. By quantifying prediction confidence and detecting out-of-distribution data, the approach aims to reduce overconfident errors and enhance the safety and robustness of AI-assisted scientific discovery.


The feature can be found here:
- [Speeding up uncertainty quantification for aerospace applications](https://aerospaceamerica.aiaa.org/year-in-review/speeding-up-uncertainty-quantification-for-aerospace-applications/)

Panagiotis Angeloudis

Associate Professor (Reader)

Dr Panagiotis Angeloudis is Reader in Transport Systems & Logistics at the Centre for Transport Engineering & Modelling (CTEM) and the Department of Civil & Environmental Engineering at Imperial College London. His research focuses on the intersection of vehicle autonomy, multi-agent systems modelling, network optimisation and their applications to fleet operations, freight and maritime transport.     (more)

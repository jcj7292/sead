Dr. Jie Chen Assistant Professor

Dr. Jie Chen is an Assistant Professor in Mechanical Engineering at Virginia Tech. He was a Postdoctoral Fellow in Mechanical Engineering at Northwestern University (IDEAL Lab) after gaining PhD from Mechanical Engineering at Arizona State University (PARA Lab). His research focuses on physics-based uncertainty-aware data-driven analysis and design under uncertainty applied in advanced manufacturing, advanced materials systems, and fatigue & fracture.     (more)
